Built on trust

Neighbourly. Secure. Reliable.

Neighbours helping neighbours works best when everyone feels safe. Here's how we make sure of it.

Verified Helpers

Every helper is ID-verified and community-rated. Read their reviews and see past tasks before anyone handles your errand. You're always in control.

Protected Payments

Your money is held in escrow and only released once you confirm the task is done. Helpers only get paid when you're happy. No cash, no risk, no stress.

Your Neighbourhood Only

Tasks are visible only to people in your area. This isn't an anonymous platform — it's your actual neighbour, someone who lives around the corner.
